Data Sources:

eu_ref_results_ni.csv drawn from EU Referendum results by constituency, available
  from the Elections Office of Northern Ireland (EONI).
  http://www.eoni.org.uk/getmedia/c371ecda-c0b7-4914-83ca-50d38b5ca8ea/EU-REFERENDUM-2016-CONSTITUENCY-COUNT-TOTALS

ni_income.csv is a subset of "Gross Annual Pay - Annual Survey of Hours and
  Earnings (administrative geographies)", a set of indicators from the Northern
  Ireland Neighbourhood Information Service (NINIS).
  http://www.ninis2.nisra.gov.uk/Download/Labour%20Market/Gross%20Annual%20Pay%20-%20Annual%20Survey%20of%20Hours%20and%20Earnings%20(administrative%20geographies).ods

ni_rel.csv is adapted from "Religion or Religion Brought Up In: KS212NI
  (administrative Geographies)", from Census 2011, accessed through NINIS.
  http://www.ninis2.nisra.gov.uk/public/PivotGrid.aspx?ds=7479&lh=73&yn=2011&sk=136&sn=Census%202011&yearfilter=2037
